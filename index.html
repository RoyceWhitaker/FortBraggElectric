Who we are

At Fort Bragg Electric, somthing - like a hook.
Founded in 1979, Fort Bragg Electric is a full-service electrical and plumbing company.

Although we have grown in size our beliefs have not changed. The goal has always been to provide the highest quality work at a competitive price and make the customer our top priority.

We are committed to performing the highest quality of work with accomplished technicians.

We are dedicated to being responsive, organized and courteous in our customer relationships.

We are resolved to maintain premiere standards of fairness and affordability in our business dealings.
